## Features

### Core Capabilities
- **AI-Powered Deal Diagnostics**: Automatically identify at-risk deals and generate actionable insights.
- **Real-Time Sales Co-Pilot**: Live call transcription with AI-powered suggestions.
- **Objection Crusher**: Generate persuasive rebuttals for common sales objections.
- **Auto Email Generator**: Create personalized follow-up emails in multiple styles.
- **HubSpot Integration**: Seamless connection to your CRM data.
- **Company Knowledge Integration**: Leverage your company's value props and case studies.

### Analytics & Insights
- **Risk Assessment**: AI-powered deal health scoring and risk indicators.
- **Engagement Tracking**: Monitor prospect engagement and activity patterns.
- **Competitor Analysis**: Detect competitor mentions and competitive threats.
- **Win Probability**: Predict deal closure likelihood with confidence scores.
- **Actionable Recommendations**: AI-generated next best actions with priority levels.

### User Experience
- **Modern UI/UX**: Clean, intuitive interface with real-time updates.
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices.
- **Real-Time Updates**: Live transcription and AI suggestions during calls.
- **Mock Mode**: Test features without API keys for demonstration.
- **One-Click Actions**: Create HubSpot tasks and send emails directly from the app.
